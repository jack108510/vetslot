The model

Why clinics discount slots —
and why that's good for you.

An empty OR costs real money

Staff are paid regardless. Equipment is booked. A cancellation that goes unfilled is dead overhead — clinics are motivated to fill it fast.

The discount comes from timing, not quality

Same licensed vet. Same equipment. Same standard of care. The price drops because the slot is last-minute — nothing else changes.

You get first access, not a leftover

Alerts go to our list before the slot is posted anywhere else. Being signed up is the only way to know about it in time.

The reality of vet pricing

"Spay and neuter procedures often range from $300–$600 at full price. Last-minute clinic openings are one of the few legitimate ways that price comes down significantly."

Pricing varies by city, clinic, and animal size. Empty Slot connects you directly with the clinic — we don't set prices.

Questions

The stuff you're
probably wondering.

Is this actually free?

Signing up and getting alerts is completely free. No subscription, no credit card required to join the list.

Are the vet clinics legitimate?

Every clinic on Empty Slot is a licensed veterinary practice. We verify credentials before any clinic can post openings. The discount is on timing, not on the quality of care.

Why would a vet discount their services?

A last-minute cancellation leaves staff paid and equipment ready but no revenue coming in. Filling that slot at a discount is still better than leaving it empty. You get a lower price; they cover their costs.

How do I claim a slot when I'm alerted?

Click the alert, review the listing, and secure the slot with a small refundable deposit (typically $25–$50). The discounted price is locked in at booking. You pay the remainder at the clinic on the day.

What if I need to cancel?

Deposits are refundable if you cancel more than 48 hours before the appointment. Each clinic's exact cancellation policy is shown clearly before you confirm.

What do you do with my email?

Your email is used only to send you slot alerts that match what you signed up for. We don't sell it, share it, or use it for anything else. One-click unsubscribe in every email.
